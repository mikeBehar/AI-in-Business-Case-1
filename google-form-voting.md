# Google Form: Case 1 Board Vote

## Form Title
Liberty Regional Bank Board Vote - LendSmart™ AI Deployment Decision

---

## Form Description
You are voting as a member of Liberty Regional Bank's Board of Directors on how to proceed with the LendSmart™ AI loan screening system. This vote will be recorded and consequences will be revealed based on the board's collective decision.

**Important**: 
- Vote based on your current thinking after hearing board discussion
- Your vote may differ from your pre-meeting position - that's okay and expected
- There are no "right" or "wrong" votes - only consequences
- Results will be shared with the class after voting closes

---

## Section 2: Your Vote

### Question 2.1: Board Vote on LendSmart™ Deployment
**Type**: Multiple choice (required)
**Question**: How do you vote on LendSmart™ deployment?

**Options**:
- ⭕ **Option 1**: Deploy as-is - Accept vendor's current system (maximum financial benefit: +$1.6M Year 1)
- ⭕ **Option 2**: Deploy with fairness constraints - Require vendor model retraining (slightly reduced savings: +$1.4M Year 1)
- ⭕ **Option 3**: Deploy with monitoring + community investment - Marketing's hybrid approach (+$1.1M Year 1 after community program costs)
- ⭕ **Option 4**: Delay 6 months - Independent audit and model fixes before deployment (+$600K Year 1, 6 months of lost opportunity)
- ⭕ **Option 5**: Reject AI solution - Continue human-only processing, invest in loan officer training (-$300K Year 1)

---

### Question 2.2: Vote Confidence
**Type**: Linear scale (required)
**Question**: How confident are you in your vote?

**Scale**: 1 (Not at all confident) to 5 (Extremely confident)

### Question 2.3: Vote Shift (Optional)
**Type**: Paragraph (optional)

### Question 3.1: Primary Decision Driver

- ⭕ Competitive positioning

- ⭕ Brand reputation and community trust

- ⭕ Prioritize caution - avoid risks even at cost of opportunity
- ⭕ Depends on specifics - can't generalize my risk approach

## Form Settings for Instructor

**Recommended Settings**:
- ✅ Limit to 1 response per person (require sign-in)
- ✅ Collect email addresses (to match with Blackboard submissions)
- ✅ Release results summary after form closes
- ✅ Allow response editing (until vote closes)
- ⏰ Set automatic closing time at end of board meeting session

**Data Export**:
- Download responses as spreadsheet for analysis
- Compare pre-meeting positions (Blackboard) with final votes (Google Form)
- Analyze vote distribution and confidence levels
- Track AI tool usage patterns

**Privacy Note**:
Inform students that aggregate voting data and anonymized quotes may be used for course discussion but individual votes will remain confidential outside the class.
